70、80后安化人的青春回忆：带着咸菜上学的那些年
Reminiscences of Anhua people born in the 70s and 80s: the years we carried pickled vegetables and took them to school

2016-01-05 网络 品味安化
January 5, 2016 | Online | Taste of Anhua

Partially translated from the original by Peter Radocchia.

生于70、80（90初）年代的农村，那时候的我们，上学时候是很艰苦的！
For those of us born in villages during 70s, 80s and even early 90s, going to school was pretty arduous!

那时家里孩子多，父母收入也不高，初中时候，家离学校远的，就寄读在学校，一周回去一次。像现在陪读啥的根本没有，能在赶集的时候捎带点吃的就挺好的了。
At that time, families had a lot of children, incomes were low, and middle school was far away. We boarded at school and came home once a week.

记得那时候，周日下午就要返校，临走前，母亲把腌好的萝卜从大缸里拿出来，洗洗切成丝，滴上一点油炒，再放上点儿辣椒，炒完了，给我装到罐头瓶里，我再塞满满一包馒头，接过几张零钱（一块的），这就是我一周的伙食。
Remember that time, Sunday afternoon will be back to school, before leaving, the mother put the marinated radish out from the big bowl, wash and cut wire, a drop of oil to fry, add some pepper, fried finished, I installed in a tin can, I stuffed with a full package Steamed Buns, took a few change (a), this is my meal a week.

那时候，家离学校远的，就寄读在学校（基本上没有家长在学校外面租房陪读这一说），都是背一坨米，带一瓶咸菜，一般只有周末才回家一次，住学校的集体宿舍，日子叫一个艰苦！
At that time, my home is far from school, a boarding school is in the (basically no parents outside the school PeiDou rent this one), is back a meter, with a bottle of pickles, usually only on weekends home, live in the school dormitory, called a hard day!

记得刚上初中那会儿，才开学时，有好多同学自己不会蒸饭，有的蒸饭不淘米，有的蒸饭不加水，有的把握不好水的多少。当时我们一间二十个平方左右的宿舍住了二十几个同学，热闹是很热闹，争吵打架被偷也是常事儿。
Just remember that the junior high school that time, after school, many students have their own not steaming, steaming rice and some don't, some rice without water, some do not grasp how much water. We had a twenty square meters of the dormitory twenty a few students are very lively, lively, fighting is the thing stolen.

最难熬的是冬天，要用冷水淘米洗饭盒，太阳出来，手上的冻疮又痛又痒！
The most difficult thing is winter, it is necessary to wash rice boxes with cold water, the sun comes out, and the frostbite on your hands is painful and itchy!

现在的孩子，好多是家长租房陪读；有些就算是住校，每顿都是在学校食堂买饭吃，根本没有自己蒸饭这一说法了。对于现在大多数城镇、城市的娃儿们来说，很多是不知道那种生活，也无法体会的。
Now the children, many parents PeiDou rent; some even live on campus, every meal is to buy food in the school canteen, no this is the own rice. For now, most of the towns, city guys, many do not know the kind of life, also can not understand.

那时候，我们背上一大周的米，自己蒸饭，然后拌上自己带的咸菜，要是零花钱够点，那么就打一份汤，要是家里给得少，那么也是很舍不得打一份汤的。
At that time, we back a week meters, then mixed with their own rice, with pickles, if enough pocket money, then hit a soup, if the house for less, it is also very reluctant to make a soup.

那时候日子过得是紧巴巴的，都是一天一天数着希望快点放周末，一般都是觉得过了星期三，那这一周就快了，然后回到家中，尝尝家里的土鸡蛋，加加餐。
When the day is tight, is a day a day of hope put on the weekend, are generally felt that this Wednesday, a week soon, and then returned home, home soil egg taste, simple meal.

那些年我们曾带过的菜
The dishes we had brought in those years

【咸菜】也就是腌萝卜切成的丝，然后放油炒一下，啃着馒头就着咸菜就能撑一周。也有榨菜、腌菜。
[salted vegetable] is the silk of pickled radish, then stir fry the oil, and eat the steamed bread with the salted vegetable for a week. There are also pickles and pickles.

【鸡蛋】这个绝对在那个时候是好东西，少不了被小伙伴们羡慕！那时候一顿都是只吃半个，根本舍不得吃多。哪像现在的孩子不吃到处扔！
[egg] this is absolutely a good thing at that time, and the envy of the partners! At that time, only half a meal was eaten at all. What's like today's kids don't eat everywhere!

【芋头干】家里一般都没有好菜可带，有些人家芋头比较多，就会晒些，到时候，家里的孩子上学就带着这个吃，挺好吃的。
[taro] families usually have no good dishes to bring, some families will have more taro, they will be basked in some time. When the children come to school, they will take this to eat, which is delicious.

【霉豆腐】一般都是冬天腌制的，真的很好吃哦！
[mycophenal tofu] is usually preserved in winter. It is really delicious.

那些年，拿两块钱就跟狗捡到骨头似的，能高兴一整天！
Those years, take two dollars to pick up a dog with a bone like, can be happy all day!
